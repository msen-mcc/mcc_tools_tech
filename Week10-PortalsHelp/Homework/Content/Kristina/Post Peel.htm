Post Peel

  1. When the time is up, neutralize your skin with the neutralizing solution.

  2. Wash your face with a gentle cleanser.

  3. Dry your face with a towel.

  4. Apply hyaluronic acid serum or another non-irritating gentle moisturizer to your face.

  5. On nights 2 and 3, apply petroleum jelly, emu oil, or argan oil to your face before bed. Your face may begin to peel or flake about day 3 and continue peeling for 7-10 days. Even if your skin does not peel or flake, the TCA peel is still effective for your skin.

  6. Wash your face twice daily and keep moisturized.

  7. Space TCA peels at least 4 weeks apart.
