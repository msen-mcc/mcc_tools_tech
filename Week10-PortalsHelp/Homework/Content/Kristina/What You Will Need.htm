What You Will Need

You should have the following ready before beginning your peel:

  • Hair clips and elastic

  • Gel cleanser

  • Towel

  • Rubbing alcohol

  • Gauze pads

  • Tissues

  • Cotton swabs

  • Petroleum jelly

  • Timer

  • Vinyl or latex gloves

  • Access to water from a sink

  • TCA peel

  • Neutralizing solution (available where at-home TCA peels are sold)

  • Gentle cleanser

  • Hyaluronic acid serum
